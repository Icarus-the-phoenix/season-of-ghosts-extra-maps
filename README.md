Several extra maps for Season of Ghosts! Included are preconfigured maps for the following:

* 6 Player House Options
* Mothers Coil (Can be used as a player house too)
* Remade Dawnstep Bridge
* Eastern Watchtower
* Matsuki's Estate (with overlay for mist)
* The Worst Puzzle

Several Macros and Journals are also included for ease of use.

Assets used for these maps are made by the awesome creators at [Forgotten Adventures](www.forgotten-adventures.net), [Crosshead Studios](https://crossheadstudios.com/), and [Tom Cartos](https://www.tomcartos.com/)! Maps were created with [Dungeon Draft](https://dungeondraft.net/)!
